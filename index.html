Our Journey

From wide-eyed 6th graders to accomplished graduates

2017

The Beginning

90 nervous but excited kids stepped into PDS Jand, not knowing they were about to meet their second family.

2018

Finding Our Footing

First year complete! Bonds started forming, inside jokes began, and we realized this was more than just a school.

2019

Making Our Mark

Batch 6 started winning competitions, setting records, and becoming known as the batch to watch.

2020

The Challenge

COVID hit, but our bonds only grew stronger through virtual classes and support during tough times.

2021-2022

Coming of Age

Senior years brought deeper friendships, leadership roles, and preparing for the world beyond school.

2023-2024

Legacy Complete

Graduation arrived with mixed emotions - pride in our achievements but sadness at parting ways.
